2026 World Cup Bracket Challenge

Leaderboard Rules & scoring

1 Group Stage

Tap two teams in a group to swap them. Rank 1st through 4th — only your 1st and 2nd score points, and your 3rd is eligible for a wildcard slot.

2 Wildcards — 8 best 3rd-place teams

Pick exactly 8 of the 12 third-place teams to advance to the Round of 32.

3 Knockout Bracket

Auto-populates from your group rankings and wildcards. Click winners through to the Final.

4 Tiebreaker

Predict your champion's average goals per game. Your champion is whoever you pick to win the Final.
